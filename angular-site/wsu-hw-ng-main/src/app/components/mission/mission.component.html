Our Mission

At Birds Gone Wild, our mission is to provide a safe haven for injured and orphaned birds, offering them the care and rehabilitation they need to regain their strength and vitality. We are dedicated to the preservation of avian life, nurturing each bird with compassion and expertise until they are ready to soar back into the wild, where they belong.

We believe that every bird deserves the chance to heal and thrive in its natural habitat. Through our efforts, we strive not only to rescue and rehabilitate but also to foster a deeper connection between people and the magnificent birds that share our world.

Our commitment extends beyond the walls of our conservatory. We work tirelessly to educate the public about the importance of conservation and the role we all play in protecting these incredible creatures. Together, we can ensure that the skies remain filled with the beauty and song of birds, for generations to come.
